# Meta-Ad-Performance-Analysis
## Project Overview
This project presents a Meta Ad Performance Analysis Dashboard built to evaluate and monitor advertising campaigns running on Facebook and Instagram.The dashboard provides a complete funnel view from impressions to purchases, along with audience, geographic, time-based, and ad-type insights to support data-driven marketing decisions.The objective is to help marketing teams track campaign effectiveness, optimize budgets, and improve conversion performance.  

## Business Objective  
• Measure ad performance across Facebook and Instagram  
• Identify high-performing platforms, ad formats, and audiences  
• Track engagement and conversion efficiency  
• Optimize budget allocation and campaign strategy  
• Provide actionable insights for improving ROI  

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)  
The analysis centers on the following core metrics:  
- **Impressions:** The total number of times ads were displayed to users, used to measure overall reach.
- **Clicks:** The number of times users clicked on the ads, indicating engagement intent.
- **Shares:** The number of times ads were shared by users, reflecting viral engagement.
- **Comments:** The number of user comments on ads, used to gauge user sentiment and feedback.
- **Purchases (Conversions):** The number of completed purchases made by customers after interacting with the ads.
- **Engagements:** The total volume of interactions, calculated as the sum of Clicks, Shares, and Comments.
- **Click Through Rate (CTR):** The percentage of impressions that resulted in clicks, used to measure ad effectiveness.
- **Engagement Rate:** The percentage of impressions that resulted in engagements, indicating overall ad appeal.
- **Conversion Rate:** The percentage of clicks that resulted in a purchase, measuring lower-funnel efficiency.
- **Purchase Rate:** The percentage of total impressions that resulted in purchases, showing conversion efficiency from reach.
- **Total Budget:** The total amount of spend allocated across all advertising campaigns.
- **Average Budget per Campaign:** The average budget allocated to each individual campaign.

## Tech Stack & Data Schema
- **Tool:**  Power BI (Data Visualization & Data Modeling).
- **Data Source:**  CSV Files consisting of four structured tables.  
- **ad_events:** Event-level logs capturing user interactions such as Impressions, Clicks, Shares, Comments, and Purchases.  
- **ads:** Ad-level metadata including platform (Facebook/Instagram), ad type, and targeting criteria.  
- **campaign:** Campaign strategy data covering budgets, start and end dates, and campaign timelines.  
- **users:** User demographic and interest data including gender, age, location, and interests.

## Key Insights
• High CTR (~11–12%) indicates strong ad creatives and targeting  
• Engagement Rate (~13.5%) shows good audience interest  
• Purchase Rate (~0.6%) is relatively low, indicating funnel drop-off  
• Females contribute the highest engagement share  
• Most responsive age group: 18–30 years  
• Top engagement regions: India, US, Brazil, Germany, UK  
• Peak engagement hours: Afternoon to Evening (15–20 hrs)  

## Ad Type Performance Summary
• Video Ads deliver the highest CTR, Engagement Rate, and Conversion Rate  
• Stories Ads perform strongly with high impressions and engagement  
• Image and Carousel Ads show stable but slightly lower conversion efficiency  

## Conclusion
This project analyzes Meta ad performance across Facebook and Instagram using key funnel metrics from impressions to purchases.
The analysis reveals strong engagement and visibility but highlights a conversion drop-off at the lower funnel.
The dashboard provides actionable insights to optimize targeting, ad formats, timing, and budget allocation for improved ROI.
